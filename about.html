From Wall Street to Main Street

With over 25 years of experience spanning financial services, technology, and product development, I bring a unique perspective to creating accessible financial analysis tools. My career has been defined by transforming complex financial concepts into user-friendly solutions that empower investors at every level.

I've spent my career bridging the gap between institutional-grade financial technology and everyday investors. After leading global technology teams managing hundreds of engineers and large budgets at a major online brokerage, I witnessed firsthand how powerful financial tools were often locked away behind expensive enterprise systems.

Entrepreneurial Journey

This experience drove me to found a retirement planning company, where I transformed an idea into a thriving business with real clients and revenue in under a year. I led every aspect of the operation—from product design and market research to client support and technology development—proving that sophisticated financial services could be delivered simply and affordably.

Why I Build These Tools

As both a developer and dividend investor, I understand your frustration with juggling multiple platforms and manual calculations. I've felt the pain of paying premium prices for basic financial data, or worse, making investment decisions with incomplete information.

My mission is simple: create add-ons and tools to help individual investors make informed decisions. Whether you're tracking a $1,000 portfolio or managing millions, you deserve the same quality of analysis tools that institutional investors take for granted.

My Commitment to You

Every line of code I write is guided by the belief that powerful financial tools should be accessible, accurate, and affordable. I'm committed to keeping core features free while continuously expanding capabilities based on real user needs.

When you use my tools, you're not just getting a Google Sheets add-on—you're getting decades of financial technology expertise packaged into solutions that work the way you think about investing.
