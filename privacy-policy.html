🔒 Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy

Effective Date: January 30, 2025
Add-on: Dividend Toolkit
Version: 1.0

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Dividend Toolkit operates with a privacy-first approach: no personal data collection, no tracking, no permanent storage of your information.

What Information We Access

Stock Symbols Only

We only access the stock ticker symbols (e.g., "AAPL", "MSFT") that you enter into our functions to fetch dividend data.

Function Results

We process the results of our dividend analysis functions within your spreadsheet environment only.

No Personal Data

We do not access, collect, or store any personal information, portfolio data, or sensitive financial information.

What We DON'T Access

  • Your personal information or Google account details
  • Your portfolio holdings or investment amounts
  • Other spreadsheet data beyond the specific cells with our functions
  • Your browsing history or behavior outside the add-on
  • Any financial account information or trading data

How We Use Information

Real-Time Data Processing

When you use our functions (e.g., =latestDividend("AAPL")), we:

  • Send the stock symbol to Yahoo Finance API to retrieve dividend data
  • Process the data within Google's secure infrastructure
  • Return the results directly to your spreadsheet cell
  • Cache results temporarily for performance optimization

Smart Caching System

To provide fast performance and reduce unnecessary API calls:

  • We temporarily cache dividend data within Google's systems
  • Cache expires automatically after a reasonable time period
  • No cached data is stored permanently or outside Google's infrastructure
  • Cache is unique to your session and not shared with other users

Data Security

Your data security is paramount:

  • Google Infrastructure: All processing occurs within Google's secure cloud environment
  • No External Storage: We don't store your data on external servers or databases
  • Encrypted Transit: All data communications use industry-standard encryption
  • Minimal Access: We request only the minimum permissions required for functionality
  • Session-Based: Any temporary data is cleared when your session ends

What We Don't Do

We explicitly do NOT:

  • Collect or store personal information
  • Track your usage patterns or behavior
  • Share data with advertisers or third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Store spreadsheet data permanently
  • Access data from other spreadsheets or applications
  • Monitor your investment decisions or portfolio performance

Third-Party Services

Yahoo Finance API

Our add-on retrieves dividend data from Yahoo Finance:

  • We send only stock ticker symbols to Yahoo Finance
  • Yahoo Finance provides publicly available market data
  • No personal information is shared with Yahoo Finance
  • Yahoo Finance usage is subject to their terms and privacy policy

Google Workspace Integration

As a Google Sheets add-on, we operate within:

  • Google's secure cloud infrastructure
  • Google Workspace security and privacy controls
  • Google's data processing and privacy policies

Your Privacy Rights

You maintain complete control:

  • Revoke Access: Uninstall the add-on at any time through Google Workspace Marketplace
  • Data Control: Your spreadsheet data remains entirely under your control
  • Transparency: All functions and their purposes are clearly documented
  • No Lock-in: Remove our functions and your data remains intact

Children's Privacy

Dividend Toolkit is designed for adult investors and financial professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from users under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has used our add-on, please contact us immediately.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect:

  • Changes in our add-on functionality
  • Updates to legal requirements
  • Improvements to our privacy practices

Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Updated effective date on this policy
  • Notification within the add-on interface
  • Email notification if we have your contact information

International Users

Dividend Toolkit operates globally through Google's infrastructure:

  • Data processing occurs within Google's global network
  • We comply with applicable international privacy regulations
  • Stock data is sourced from Yahoo Finance's global service
