🔧 Function Library

Complete Dividend Analysis Toolkit

Professional-grade functions that bring Wall Street analytics to your spreadsheets

=latestDividend("AAPL")
Get the most recent dividend payment amount for any stock
=lastDividendDate("MSFT")
Retrieve the exact date of the last dividend payment
=ytdDividend("JNJ")
Calculate total dividends paid year-to-date
=trailingAnnualDividend("KO")
Sum of all dividends paid in the last 12 months
=forwardAnnualDividend("PG")
Projected annual dividend based on current trends
=trailingDividendYield("T")
Dividend yield calculated from 12-month historical data
=getDividendFrequency("MMM")
Automatically detect payment frequency (monthly, quarterly, etc.)
=nextEstimatedDividendDate("IBM")
Predict the next dividend payment date using AI algorithms
=estimatedNextDividend("WMT")
Estimate the amount of the next dividend payment
=dividendGrowthTrend("HD")
Calculate dividend growth rate and identify trends
=getCurrentPrice("GOOGL")
Real-time stock price for yield calculations
=lastExDividendDate("V")
Get the most recent ex-dividend date for timing analysis
