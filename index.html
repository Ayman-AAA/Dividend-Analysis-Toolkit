Available Functions

Complete toolkit of dividend analysis functions ready to use in your spreadsheets

=latestDividend("AAPL")
Most recent dividend payment amount
=lastDividendDate("MSFT")
Date of the last dividend payment
=ytdDividend("JNJ")
Total dividends paid year-to-date
=trailingAnnualDividend("KO")
Total dividends in the last 12 months
=forwardAnnualDividend("PG")
Estimated annual dividend projection
=trailingDividendYield("T")
Dividend yield based on 12-month data
=getDividendFrequency("MMM")
Payment frequency detection
=nextEstimatedDividendDate("IBM")
Predict next dividend payment date
=estimatedNextDividend("WMT")
Estimate next dividend amount
=dividendGrowthTrend("HD")
Calculate dividend growth rate
=getCurrentPrice("GOOGL")
Current stock price information
=lastExDividendDate("V")
Estimated ex-dividend date
