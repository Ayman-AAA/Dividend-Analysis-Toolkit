1. Service Description

Dividend Analysis Toolkit is a Google Sheets add-on that provides comprehensive dividend analysis and stock data integration tools. The service connects to Yahoo Finance APIs and other financial data sources to deliver dividend information and analysis directly into your spreadsheets. The service includes:

  • Real-time and historical dividend data retrieval for publicly traded stocks
  • Historical dividend analysis including YTD and trailing annual dividend calculations
  • Dividend yield calculations (current, trailing, and forward-looking estimates)
  • Dividend frequency detection and payment schedule analysis
  • Dividend growth trend analysis and future payment estimations
  • Current stock price integration for yield calculations
  • Custom functions accessible through standard Google Sheets formulas
  • Financial analysis tools and interactive dashboards
